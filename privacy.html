Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 24, 2026 · TCG Tools

This Privacy Policy explains what information TCG Tools collects, how it is used, and your choices. By using the app you agree to this policy.

Information we collect

Account information: when you sign in (e.g. with Google), we receive a basic profile (name, email, a unique account ID) used to create and secure your account.

App data: decks you create, settings, and profile details you enter are stored to provide the service.

Purchase information: subscriptions are processed by the app store and our payment provider (RevenueCat). We receive your subscription status, not your full payment details.

Analytics & crash data: we use Google Firebase Analytics to collect a small set of product events — for example when an account is created, a sign-in occurs, onboarding is completed or skipped, the subscription screen is viewed, and a subscription purchase completes — along with screen views and a general free/pro account-type property. We also use Google Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash reports, stack traces, and basic device and operating-system information so we can diagnose and fix problems. This data is used to understand usage and improve the app. The app contains no ads and no advertising identifiers, and we do not use this data to build advertising profiles.

How we use information

To provide and maintain the app, manage your account and subscription, respond to support requests, measure and improve features, diagnose crashes, and keep the service secure. We do not sell your personal information.

Third-party services

We use trusted providers to run the app, including Google Firebase (authentication, data storage, analytics, and crash reporting), RevenueCat (payments and subscription management), and the app stores. Card images and card data are retrieved from public TCG data sources. Each provider processes data under its own privacy policy.

Data retention & deletion

We keep your data while your account is active. You can delete your account from the profile screen. After you request deletion, your data is retained for 30 days (so you can recover your account if you change your mind) and then permanently removed. Active subscriptions must be cancelled separately in your app store account to stop future billing.

Children's privacy

The app is not directed to children under the age required by your local law to consent to data processing. We do not knowingly collect data from such children.

Your rights

Depending on your region, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise them, contact us at tcgtoolsofficial@gmail.com.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. Material changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above and, where appropriate, an in-app notice.

Contact
