Account & Data Deletion

Last updated: June 30, 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of your TCG Tools account and the data associated with it. TCG Tools is developed by Polar Bear Studios.

How to request deletion

To request that your account and associated data be deleted, email us from the email address associated with your TCG Tools account:

Send a deletion request to:

tcgtoolsofficial@gmail.com

Subject: "Account deletion request" — and include the email address or handle on your TCG Tools account so we can locate it.

We will verify that the request comes from the account owner and then process the deletion. You do not need to be a paying subscriber to request deletion.

What gets deleted

When your deletion request is processed, the following data tied to your account is permanently removed:

  • Your account identity (email, username/handle, profile photo)
  • Your saved decks and any deck test results
  • Your profile data and in-app activity (e.g. progress counters)
  • Your authentication record (email/password and/or Google sign-in link)

What may be retained, and for how long

Data Retention
Account & profile data, saved decks, activity Deleted within 30 days of a verified request.
Backups containing residual copies Purged on the normal backup rotation, no later than 90 days.
Records we are legally required to keep (e.g. transaction or tax records for purchases made through Google Play) Retained only as required by law, then deleted.

Subscription billing is handled by Google Play. Cancelling a subscription is done through your Google Play account; deleting your TCG Tools account does not by itself cancel an active Google Play subscription.

Questions

For any questions about account deletion or your data, contact us at tcgtoolsofficial@gmail.com. See also our Privacy Policy.
